## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Mehmet Can Bil Karamalli ("we," "us," or "our"), the developer of Post Oven ("the Application"), collects, uses, stores, and shares information about you ("User," "you," or "your") when you download, install, and use the Application.

## 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Account Information:**
When you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password. We do not store your password in plain text.

**Company Profile Information:**
When you create a company profile within the Application, you may provide: company name, industry, tone of voice, brand colors, company logo, website URL, phone number, address, company description, and key product descriptions. This information is used solely to power AI content generation tailored to your brand.

**Reference Images:**
You may upload images to the Application to serve as style references for AI-generated posts. These images are stored on our servers and used solely for the purpose of generating content at your request.

**Post Topic and Edit Instructions:**
When generating posts, you provide text inputs such as a topic description (up to 500 characters) or edit instructions for refining generated images. These inputs are processed to generate your requested content.

**Display Name:**
You may optionally provide a display name associated with your account.

### 1.2 Information Generated Through Use of the Application

**Generated Content:**
The Application generates AI-powered captions, hashtags, and images based on your inputs. These generated posts are stored in your account history and associated with your account.

**Credit Transaction Records:**
We maintain records of your credit balance, credit grants (including the reason, such as sign-up, subscription, or purchase), credit deductions, and the associated operations (e.g., which generation mode was used).

**Post Generation Metadata:**
When you generate a post, we store metadata including the aspect ratio, generation mode, generation timestamp, and company profile used.

### 1.3 Information Collected Automatically

**Technical Data:**
When you use the Application, we may automatically collect certain technical information necessary for the Application to function, such as your device's operating system type and version, app version, and error logs. This information is used solely for debugging and improving the Application.

**Authentication Tokens:**
The Application uses JSON Web Tokens (JWTs) to authenticate your sessions. These tokens are generated upon login and used to verify your identity with our server for each request.

### 1.4 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:
- Precise geolocation data
- Device contacts, call logs, or SMS data
- Biometric data
- Social media account credentials or data (we do not integrate with social media accounts)
- Payment card numbers or banking information (all payment processing is handled entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store on our behalf; we receive only a notification that a purchase was completed)

Technical data and usage patterns (aggregated and anonymized where possible) may be used to diagnose issues, fix bugs, and improve the Application's features and performance.

**To Ensure Security and Prevent Fraud:**
We use technical data and authentication information to protect the security of the Application and to detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity.

**To Comply With Legal Obligations:**
We may use or disclose your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or enforceable governmental requests.

## 3. HOW WE STORE AND PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION

### 3.1 Data Storage

All user data — including account information, company profiles, reference images, generated posts, and credit records — is stored on servers hosted in the United States. We operate our own self-hosted infrastructure; your data is not shared with any third-party database-as-a-service provider's shared infrastructure.

### 3.2 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Application's services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data and generated content from our systems within a commercially reasonable time period, except where we are required to retain data by applicable law. Anonymized or aggregated data that cannot identify you may be retained indefinitely.

You may delete individual generated posts or reference images at any time through the Application's interface. You may delete your entire account — and all associated data — at any time through the Application's privacy and security settings.

### 3.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) between the Application and our servers
- Password hashing — passwords are never stored in plain text
- JWT-based authentication with cryptographic validation for all server requests
- Server-side access controls and input validation to prevent unauthorized data access

However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

We receive only a notification from the platform confirming that a purchase has been made, along with the product purchased and the associated user account, for the purpose of crediting your account.

### 4.3 No Other Third-Party Data Sharing

Beyond the services described above, we do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal data with any other third parties for their own marketing or commercial purposes.
